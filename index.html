From My Heart

To Jedidiah, the one this world couldn’t deserve

This site isn’t just for you. It is you.
Every color, every corner, every word I built this because I needed a place where my love for you could breathe.

You are more than just the woman I love.
You are the gentlest silence in my loudest thoughts.
You are the reason I wake up with a steadier heart and fall asleep with something to dream about.

You’re the storm and the stillness, the ache and the answer.
You walked into my life without asking for space, but somehow, you made room for light in every part of me.

If there was a language strong enough to hold how I feel about you, I would speak only that.
If there was a place far enough to escape this world with you, I’d take you there and never look back.

But here’s something I need to say honestly
You might not hear from me for a while.

Not because my love has faded.
Not because anything changed.
But because sometimes life pulls us in directions we didn’t plan for.
And I need you to know that even when I’m gone from the screen or silent for days,
you are still the first thought that touches my mind when I wake up
and the last whisper my soul holds onto when I fall asleep.

This space is my promise.
When the words can’t reach you, this will.

So read this when you miss me.
Look around and remember: I didn’t build this for the world.
I built it for you.
